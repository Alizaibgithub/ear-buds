Returns & Refunds

Learn about our return policy, refunds, and exchange process

Return Policy

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached.

Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item, contact our support team. We will guide you through the exchange process and provide a replacement or store credit.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items, like custom products, accessories once opened, or used items, cannot be returned or refunded.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact our customer support with your order number and reason for return. We will provide a prepaid shipping label if applicable.
