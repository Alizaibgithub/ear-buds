FAQs

Find quick answers to common questions and get the information you need right away.

What is the battery life?

The earbuds offer up to 8 hours of continuous playback on a single charge, with an additional 24 hours provided by the charging case.

Is this product water-resistant?

Yes, these earbuds have an IPX4 water resistance rating, protecting against sweat and light rain.

Does it support noise cancellation?

Yes, this model features Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) technology that blocks up to 90% of ambient noise.

What devices are compatible?

Compatible with all Bluetooth-enabled devices including iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac. Bluetooth 5.0 ensures stable connectivity up to 10 meters.

What's included in the box?

The package includes: Earbuds, Charging case, USB-C charging cable, 3 pairs of ear tips (S/M/L), User manual, and Warranty card.
