FAQs

Find quick answers to common questions and get the information you need right away.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within Pakistan. International orders may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused products in original packaging. Please contact our support team to initiate a return and receive a prepaid shipping label.

Do your earbuds have a warranty?

Yes, all our products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranty options are available at checkout.

Are your earbuds water-resistant?

Most of our earbuds feature IPX4 or higher water resistance rating, protecting against sweat and light rain. Check individual product specifications for exact ratings.
