Shipping & Delivery

Get clear information about our delivery process, shipping charges, and estimated arrival times.

Shipping Time

We process all orders within 1–2 business days, and most deliveries arrive within 3–7 business days depending on your location and courier availability.

Shipping Charges

Orders above PKR 2,000 are delivered free of cost, while a standard delivery fee of PKR 199 applies to orders below this amount.

Order Tracking

After your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via SMS or email so you can monitor your delivery status anytime.

Delivery Coverage

We deliver to all major cities and remote regions across Pakistan, ensuring reliable shipping no matter where you are located.

Possible Delays

Deliveries may take additional time during holidays, weather disruptions, or courier issues, but we always keep you informed if delays occur.

Incorrect Address

If your shipping address contains any errors, please contact us immediately; orders already dispatched cannot be updated or redirected.

Damaged or Missing Items

If your order arrives damaged or an item is missing, please notify us within 48 hours with photos so we can resolve the issue quickly.
